This dashboard tracks every internal grant awarded by the RDO across fiscal years. It is designed to answer: where does the money go, is it being spent on time, what came out of it (publications), and did those PIs win external funding as a result.

What the tabs do

Quick reference for each view
Overview Portfolio snapshot. Total committed, spent, unique PIs, publications count, external follow-on funding, and the ROI ratio. Click any KPI to drill into the tab that owns that number.
Financials Money view. Committed vs spent per program, spend categories, and the At-Risk table (Active awards below 40% utilization with less than 90 days to the end date). Report compliance donut shows Overdue reports.
ROI & Follow-on External grants won by PIs after receiving an internal award. Ratio per program, sponsors ranked by total captured, full ledger of external grants.
Awards Ledger Every internal grant. Sortable, filterable, exportable. Click any row to expand its linked publications and follow-on grants inline.
Publications Publications tagged to a specific internal award, so we can attribute research output back to funded seed money.
PI Leaderboard Faculty ranked by downstream impact. Click any PI to open their full profile: awards, pubs, and follow-on funding together.
Data & Import Batch import CSV or XLSX exports, edit the program catalog, backup or reset.

How ROI is calculated

Definitions and formulas used across the tabs
ROI Ratio
External follow-on $ / Internal award $
Higher is better. A ratio of 5x means the RDO seeded 1 dollar and the PI won 5 dollars in external funding afterward.
Utilization
Spent to date / Award amount
Green when at or above 70%, amber 40 to 69%, red below 40%. At-Risk table flags Active awards that are below 40% with under 90 days left.
Impact Score (PI Leaderboard)
(Publications x 100,000) + Follow-on $
A weighted composite so PIs with publications and follow-on funding are both ranked meaningfully. The multiplier is calibrated so 1 publication roughly equals $100K of follow-on grant capture.
Assumption to confirm with VP
Currently we attribute ANY follow-on grant a PI wins to the internal seed. Once VP confirms attribution rules (e.g. only grants within 3 years post-award, or only those citing the seed grant), this formula will tighten.

How data gets updated

Data flow, sources of truth, and who owns each field

Awards + Spend

Source: InfoReady + Hana's Financial Tracker
Today: Manual batch import via the Data tab. Export from InfoReady (Report -> Application List) and drop the CSV into the importer.
Cadence: Weekly on Mondays. Any PI can request a mid-week refresh through Kelly.
Owner: Kelly (admin) runs the weekly import. Hana verifies spend numbers against her Financial Tracker sheet.
Planned P1: Direct InfoReady API pull, no manual export. Blocked on license confirmation.

Publications

Source: PI quarterly reports
Today: Add manually via Publications tab, tagged to the parent award record.
Cadence: Quarterly PI progress reports. Overdue reports show on the Financials tab.
Owner: PIs submit, Jay (GRA) enters into the tracker.
Planned P2: Auto-pull from Symplectic Elements. Citation counts refresh nightly from Google Scholar API.

Follow-on Grants

Source: PI reports + SPO system
Today: Add manually via ROI tab or Follow-on modal. Link each external grant to the internal award that seeded it.
Cadence: Whenever a PI reports it or Kelly finds it in the SPO system.
Owner: Kelly (admin).
Planned P3: Nightly reconcile from SPO proposal database, matched on PI + date.

Interactive features

Everything you can click on
• FY pills at the top scope every tab to that fiscal year.
• Filter bar under the FY pills stacks with the FY filter. Search box matches PI, record ID, program, department, college.
• Click any chart bar or slice and it becomes a filter chip. Look at the yellow drill-through banner to see what is active.
• Click any PI name anywhere on the dashboard to open the full PI profile.
• Click any row in the Awards Ledger to expand its publications and follow-on grants inline.
• Click a KPI card on Overview to jump to the tab that owns that metric.
• Click sortable column headers (the ones with the arrow icon) to sort tables.
• Sidebar hamburger collapses the nav to give you more chart room.

Roles and access

Who can see and change what
Admin Hana, Kelly, Jay. Full CRUD on awards, publications, follow-on grants. Can import and reset data.
Viewer Eric, Jill, VP. Read-only across all tabs. Can drill and filter but cannot add, edit, or delete records.
Note: Current sign-in is a local demo with hard-coded credentials. Production build will use UMD SSO once wired to the RDO backend.

Field glossary

Common terms across records
Record ID Unique identifier for one internal award, format INT-FYxx-nnn.
Program Which internal grant program funded this record (ISRCA, TIP, MPI, etc.).
Committed Total amount awarded to the PI up front.
Spent How much has actually been drawn down against the award.
Report Status Progress report cycle. Overdue means the PI has missed a due date.
Fiscal Year The FY in which the award was granted. Currently calendar-year based (FY26 = 2026).
Follow-on Grant An external grant a PI won AFTER receiving an internal award, credited to that seed.
College Code UMD short code, e.g. ENGR, CMNS, BSOS, ARHU. Full list in the Add Award modal.
